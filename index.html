General Instructions
  1. Click on slide to move it under the microscope
  2. Click on stirer to stir the solution
  3. Click on beaker to get some sample solution over the slide
  4. To move the slide under the microscope just click on it
Microscope View Instructions
  1. To open the microscope view, click on microscope image
  2. To align eye piece micrometer with stage micrometer you can use arrow keys
  3. To exit out of the microscope view press "Esc" key on keyboard
